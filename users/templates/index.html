About Us

The Campus Library Management System is a project aimed at developing a computerized system to manage all the daily work of the library. This project has many features which are generally not available in manual library management systems. It provides faculty and admin login through which the admin can monitor the entire system. It has the ability to handle the borrowing and returning of books. The system enforces a borrowing limit, typically allowing users to check out up to 1 books at a time. This limit ensures fair access to resources and prevents over-borrowing by individual users. The automated tracking of due dates and the calculation of fines for late submissions are also features. This helps encourage students and faculty members to return borrowed materials on time, thus enhancing the availability of resources for all users. The fine calculation is based on predefined rules, such as the number of days overdue and the type of material borrowed. .
